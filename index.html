Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my sprinter service? +

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for standard service. For special events, holidays, or large group transportation, we suggest booking 1-2 weeks ahead to ensure vehicle availability. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What airports do you serve in New York? +

We provide transportation to and from all New York area airports including John F. Kennedy International (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), Newark Liberty International (EWR), Teterboro Airport (TEB), Westchester County Airport (HPN). Our flight tracking service monitors your arrival and adjusts pickup times automatically.

Are your prices all-inclusive? +

Yes, all quoted prices include the base fare, fuel surcharge, tolls, taxes, and standard gratuity. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges. For airport pickups, we include 60 minutes of complimentary wait time for domestic flights and 90 minutes for international arrivals.

Do you offer corporate accounts? +

Absolutely. We offer corporate accounts with consolidated billing, dedicated account management, custom booking portals, and preferred rates. Contact us to discuss your organization's transportation needs and set up a corporate account.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee. For special events and large group bookings, please refer to your confirmation email for specific cancellation terms.
