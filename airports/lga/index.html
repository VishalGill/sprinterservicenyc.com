About LaGuardia Airport

LaGuardia Airport serves millions of passengers annually, making it one of the busiest airports in New York. The airport offers flights to destinations across the United States and internationally, with service from major carriers and regional airlines alike.

The airport is located 8 mi from downtown New York, with typical travel times of 25-50 min depending on traffic conditions and your specific destination. Rush hour traffic, special events, and construction can significantly impact travel times, which is why our chauffeurs continuously monitor conditions and plan routes accordingly.

Terminal Information

Understanding the terminal layout at LGA helps ensure smooth pickups and drop-offs. Our chauffeurs are intimately familiar with every terminal, but here's helpful information for passengers:

  • Arrivals: Most domestic arrivals use the lower level for ground transportation. International arrivals must first clear customs before proceeding to the arrivals area.
  • Departures: The departures level is typically on the upper level. We'll drop you at the entrance closest to your airline's check-in counters.
  • Private Aviation: For private/charter flights, we provide direct tarmac or FBO access where permitted.

Tips for LGA Travelers

Based on our extensive experience at LGA, here are some tips to make your journey smoother:

  • Allow Extra Time: Security lines at LGA can be lengthy, especially during peak travel periods. We recommend arriving 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international departures.
  • Share Your Flight Details: When booking, provide your airline and flight number so we can track your arrival in real-time.
  • Confirm Pickup Location: For arrivals, confirm with your chauffeur exactly where to meet—usually baggage claim for the first checked bag or outside arrivals if you have carry-on only.
  • Keep Contact Handy: Save our dispatch number and your chauffeur's contact in your phone for easy communication.
  • Consider Round-Trip: Booking round-trip service ensures reliable transportation for both legs of your journey and often includes a discount.

Frequently Asked Questions - LGA Transportation

Where will my chauffeur meet me at LGA? +

Your chauffeur will meet you inside the terminal at the baggage claim area, holding a sign with your name. If you prefer, we can also arrange curbside pickup—just let us know when booking.

What if my flight is delayed? +

No need to worry or even call us. We track your flight in real-time and automatically adjust your pickup time based on your actual arrival. There's no charge for delays.

How much luggage can I bring? +

Our executive sedans accommodate 3 passengers with 3 regular suitcases. SUVs can handle 6 passengers with 6 bags. For larger groups or excess luggage, our Sprinter vans offer ample space. If you have special cargo needs, please mention this when booking.

Do you provide child car seats? +

Yes, we can provide infant seats, toddler seats, and booster seats upon request at no additional charge. Please specify your needs when making your reservation.

Can I make a stop on the way from LGA? +

Absolutely. Additional stops can be added to your transfer for a small fee. Popular requests include stops at hotels, offices, or restaurants. Just mention your requirements when booking.
